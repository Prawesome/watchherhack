About The Hackthon

PEHIA is a non-profit initiative that believes in empowering gender minorities through Information technology. It has come to our notice that the female participation at technical events, especially Hackathons are very meagre and poor.

In association with Kerala Startup Mission, PEHIA under its WOMENDEV project intends to boost the woman participation, and to provide a safe space for them to start their journey in hackathons and entrepreneurship , by initiating a two day WOMEN only Hackathon at KTIZ, Cochin, India. VENUE

Kerala Technology Innovation Zone (KTIZ)

Kinfra Hi-Tech Park,, HMT Colony P.O., Kalamassery, Kochi, Kerala 683503

Kerala Technology Innovation Zone is envisioned as a one stop shop for technology innovation, where everything, from the Conceptualizing an idea, to its incubation, to mentor support for setting up a successful enterprise and connect to angel investors ready to invest in a bankable idea, is present. The K-TIZ shall act as a self-sustained startup ecosystem which serves the interests of the young innovators and entrepreneurs, so that all the time and energy that they have goes into product development. Moreover KTIZ will host diverse sectors under one physical location which will encourage crosspollination of ideas and is expected to trigger innovative new products that can be used for the social good.

FAQ

Do I have to know hacking to attend a hackthon?

No, you don't. Hack means to solve a problem in jury-rig manner or to improvise something which may or maynot be elegant but is defintely effective. Hackthons are places where a lot of people gather together to solve different problems in different innovative ways. So, no you don't need to know secuirty hacking for a hackthon.

I am just a beginner in coding. Can I attend?

Watch her hack 1.0 is aimed at beginner to intermediate she-coders. So you needn't worry about how techy you are to attend Watch her Hack 1.0

Which Language should I use?

Hackthons are not language specific. They are problem specific. SO choose a language you are comfortabl with.

Who all can attend the hackthon?

Undergraduate or Gradute women students, women proffesionals, women on carrer break.
